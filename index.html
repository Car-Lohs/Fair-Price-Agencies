About Us

Welcome to Fair Price Agencies, your one-stop shop for all your textile and upholstery needs.We are delighted to have you visit our website and we look forward to serving all your crafting and home decor needs. Our shop specializes in providing high-quality materials for a wide range of projects, from luxurious silk for evening wear to durable canvas for outdoor furniture. In our upholstery department, you'll find a team of experienced professionals ready to help you find the perfect fabric for your furniture and provide custom upholstery services. Whether you're looking to update an old piece of furniture or give a new one a custom look, we have the expertise to help you create the perfect look. In addition to fabrics and textiles, we also offer a wide selection of crafting supplies, including thread, buttons, and other notions, as well as home decor items such as pillows, curtains, and tablecloths. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to offer suggestions and help you find just what you need. At our shop, we believe that quality and customer service are of the utmost importance. We strive to provide both in everything we do, whether you're a professional designer or a hobbyist. We have been in the business for more than 20 years and have built a reputation for providing quality products at fair prices. We invite you to come and visit us in the center of the city, where we will be more than happy to assist you in finding the perfect materials for your next project. Don't hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions or need any assistance. We look forward to serving you!
